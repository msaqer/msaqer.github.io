Brief Bio

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Math & Computer Science at Minot State University, with expertise in machine learning, data mining, and cybersecurity. My research focuses on developing analytical tools to evaluate social media discussions on cybersecurity incidents, enhancing how digital platforms manage cyber threat narratives.
